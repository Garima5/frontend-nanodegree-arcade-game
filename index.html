Its Play Time!

To play the game:

Navigate on the canvas using up, down and side keys.Avoid the bugs and collect the gems for good score.Reach the water to resest the game

The Key elements in the game are:

The bugs which you have to prevent from colliding else score will be reduced by 1 and player's position is reset The blue gem-If caught adds 5 points to the score The green gem-If caught adds 3 points to score On reaching the water 5 is added to score and position of player is reset
